Getting Started

There are three steps to including comments in XML:

  1. Inherit XmlAnnotate on classes that are serialized.
  2. Alter serialization code to use XmlCommentWriter.
  3. Use the XmlComment attribute and specify other settings.
When it's not possible or desired to follow steps 1 and 2, consider these alternatives.
